About Me

I am a software developer located in the Atlanta, Ga area. Prior to becoming a software developer, I taught middle school math, engineering, and English as a second language to students ranging from preschool age up to retired adults. I lived abroad and have taught students in China, Japan, and the United Arab Emirates as well as in the USA.

My passion for design and problem solving led me to obtain a degree in architecture and pursue hobbies in surface pattern design and creating hand painted handbags. Ultimately, I found my way to software development as the prime outlet for my creativity, problems solving, and design skills.
